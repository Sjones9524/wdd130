Divorce

We have all heard that upward of fifty percent of marriages end in divorce. The reasons for this are numerous.

✔ One

Traditionally, one or both parties would obtain a lawyer, who would then make a case and present that case to the courts. This process can be costly and time consuming; the average time for a divorce is twelve months!

✔ Two

Mediated divorces are usually accomplished faster and for less money.

✔ Three

Those who are looking for a mediated divorce are looking for a mostly amicable separation without the need for attorneys.
