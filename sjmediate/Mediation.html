Mediation

What is divorce mediation? As a couple, concluding that it would be better off to be divorced instead of together is difficult. Regardless of the circumstances that brought this along it is one of the most financially and emotionally stressful times in someone’s life. Mediation is here to help couples looking to split to achieve the goals that they would like without the monetary cost of lawyers and the time it takes to go through the justice system.

✔ One

Mediation allows for a mutual separation of marital assets and determine (if applicable) how to effectively co-parent. This is not to say that there will not be complications on the way, but as you work with a mediator all the issues and stresses involved with this can be resolved.

✔ Two

This process can be difficult, and there needs to be someone there to help couples navigate this with minimal conflict. What is great about mediation is that while the laws surrounding divorce, because mediation is agreed upon there are certain aspects of divorce that can be amended.

✔ Three

Mediation is a great option of those people who are wanting to separate in a more positive manner.
